About us
Our story
Welcome to Paws&Claws Palace - Your Trusted Partner in Pet Care! At , Paws&Claws Palace we understand that your pets are not just animals—they are cherished members of your family. That's why we are committed to providing exceptional veterinary care and building lasting relationships with both pets and their owners. Our experienced team of compassionate veterinarians and dedicated staff is passionate about ensuring the health and well-being of your furry companions. With state-of-the-art facilities and advanced medical equipment, we offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the unique needs of your pets. We believe in a holistic approach to pet care, emphasizing preventive medicine, early detection, and personalized treatment plans. From routine wellness exams and vaccinations to advanced diagnostics, surgery, and emergency care, we strive to deliver the highest standard of care at every visit.
Our location
Located in the vibrant city of Ottawa, P&C Palace is conveniently situated at 98 Fraid Street, providing easy access for pet owners in the local community and surrounding areas. Our central location ensures that you can find us with ease, making it simple to bring your furry companions to receive the exceptional veterinary care they deserve. Whether you're a long-time resident or a new arrival to Ottawa, we are excited to serve you and your pets at our clinic on Main Street. Come visit us and experience the highest standard of compassionate and personalized pet care in the heart of Ottawa.
